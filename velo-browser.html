Features

Everything you need in one pane

Tab Bar

Multiple tabs with favicons, drag-to-reorder, and keyboard shortcuts. Cockpit chrome above the page.

Find in Page

Cmd+F opens an inline glass search pill. Highlights all matches, jumps with Enter.

Sidebar

Slide-out panel for history, bookmarks, notes and downloads. Swipe or click the edge to toggle.

Bookmarks

Star any page and retrieve it instantly from the sidebar. Folder organisation with drag-to-sort.

Notes

Persistent per-page notes in the sidebar. Markdown rendering and syntax highlighting.

Downloads

Download queue in the sidebar with progress bars and open-on-complete. WebKit handles the heavy lifting.
