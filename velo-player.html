Features

Music the way it should look

Vinyl Record

Spinning vinyl disc with concentric groove rings. Spins at 33⅓ RPM when playing, stops when paused.

Spotify API

Full Spotify Web API integration. Browse library, control playback, search and manage playlists.

Playlist Sidebar

Browse your Spotify playlists and liked songs in a glass panel. Click to queue and play instantly.

Seek Bar

Animated seek bar with smooth CSS transitions. Click anywhere to jump to that position in the track.

Track Search

Full Spotify search with live results. Type an artist, track or album and results appear instantly.

Volume Control

Integrated volume slider. Syncs with Spotify Connect — control any device in your home.
